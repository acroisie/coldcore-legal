# ColdCore — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 5, 2026**

## Overview

ColdCore is a breathing and cold exposure training app developed by Arnaud Croisier. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data
- **Sign in with Apple**: We receive your Apple ID user identifier and, optionally, your name and email (only if you choose to share them). This data is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud to your other devices.
- **Guest Mode**: If you use the app as a guest, no account data is collected.

### Health Data
- **Apple Health Integration** (optional): If you enable Apple Health, ColdCore writes mindful minutes and cold exposure sessions to the Health app. We do not read any health data from other apps. This data stays on your device and in your iCloud Health account.

### Session Data
- **Breathing Sessions**: Duration, retention times, session type, and settings are stored locally and synced via iCloud.
- **Cold Exposure Sessions**: Duration, water temperature, and exposure type are stored locally and synced via iCloud.

### Profile Data
- **Profile Photo** (optional): Stored locally and synced via iCloud. Compressed to under 500 KB.

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide the app's core functionality (session tracking, history, statistics)
- To sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
- To display instructor sessions (fetched from our CloudKit public database)

## Data Storage

All personal data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. We do not operate any external servers. We do not have access to your personal data.

Instructor session content (titles, descriptions, settings) is stored in a public CloudKit database and is visible to all users.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third party. We do **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Tracking

ColdCore does **not** track you. We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or browsing history. We do not use any tracking technologies.

## Your Rights

- **Delete your data**: Uninstalling the app removes all local data. iCloud data can be deleted via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.
- **Sign out**: You can sign out at any time from the Profile tab, which clears your local session.
- **Export**: Your session history is visible in Apple Health if you enabled the integration.

## Children's Privacy

ColdCore does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not contain age-restricted content.
