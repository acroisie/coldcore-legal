# BreatheToo — Your Privacy Choices

**Last updated: April 15, 2026**

## Your Data, Your Control

BreatheToo is designed with privacy at its core. All your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. Here are the choices you have regarding your data.

## What You Can Control

### Account

| Choice | How | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Use as Guest** | Tap "Continue as Guest" on sign-in screen | No Apple ID is collected. All data stays local. |
| **Sign in with Apple** | Tap "Sign in with Apple" | We receive only your Apple ID identifier. Name and email are optional. |
| **Sign out** | Profile tab > Sign Out | Clears your local session. Your data remains in iCloud until you delete it. |

### Health Data

| Choice | How | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Enable Apple Health** | Profile tab > Apple Health toggle | BreatheToo writes mindful minutes and cold exposure sessions to Apple Health. |
| **Disable Apple Health** | Same toggle, turn off | BreatheToo stops writing to Apple Health. Existing Health data is not deleted (managed by Apple Health). |

### Session Data

| Choice | How | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Delete a session** | History tab > swipe left on a session | Session is removed from your device and iCloud. |
| **Delete all data** | Uninstall the app | All local data is removed. |
| **Delete iCloud data** | Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage > BreatheToo | Removes all synced data from iCloud. |

### Profile

| Choice | How | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Add/remove profile photo** | Profile tab > tap photo | Photo is stored locally and synced via iCloud. Removing it deletes it everywhere. |

### Notifications

| Choice | How | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily reminder** | Profile tab > Daily Reminder toggle | Enables/disables local notifications. No data is sent to any server. |

## What We Don't Collect

- No location data
- No device identifiers or fingerprinting
- No browsing history
- No advertising data
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- No data shared with or sold to anyone

## Data Processing

All data processing happens on your device. We do not operate external servers. The only network communication is:

- **iCloud sync**: Your private data syncs between your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure.
- **Instructor sessions**: Session templates from instructors are fetched from a public CloudKit database (read-only, no personal data involved).

## Your Rights Under GDPR / CCPA

If you are in the European Union or California, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data (all data is visible in the app)
- **Delete** your personal data (see instructions above)
- **Port** your data (Apple Health export, session history visible in app)
- **Object** to processing (disable any feature via the toggles above)

Since all data is stored in your personal iCloud account and we have no access to it, exercising these rights is done directly through your device settings.
